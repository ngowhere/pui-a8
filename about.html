About Me




Hi I'm Emily Ngo

Hi I’m Emily Ngo and I’m an artist. I’m passionate about the intersection of art and technology. I’m a trained painter currently learning about developing art using code. All of my coding projects are developed using p5.js. I like exploring procedural generation, unique ways to encode information, and creative uses of color.

When I’m not coding, I’m an information systems major at Carnegie Mellon University pursuing a minor in human computer interaction. My goal is to develop meaningful, usable, and delightful applications and experiences for the everyday person.
